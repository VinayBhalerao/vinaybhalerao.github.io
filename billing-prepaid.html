Prepaid billing in 3scale system

Developer Signs up on a Free plan

Oct 9

Upgrades to paid plan

The developer decides to upgrade from free to paid plan of $200.

Oct 20

Invoices are generated

By the end of same day, invoices are generated. The invoices are kept in finalized state for provider to review. The generated invoice is based upon pro-rata calculation of the remainder of the month i.e (11/30*200) = $73.33

The variable costs are also calculated if pricing rules are defined on the method/metrics

Note: The variable costs usage will be included in next month billing cycle

Oct 21

Invoices are issued

After two days, the invoices are issued to the email address of the developer

Oct 23

Invoices are charged

After two days, the developers credit card is charged for the issued invoice

Oct 25
